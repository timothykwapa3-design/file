Who We Are

Born from the Underground

Olympus Garments was born from the belief that fashion is a language — and everyone deserves a voice. We source inspiration from underground art, global street culture, and local craftspeople, channeling their energy into limited-edition drops that mean something.

What We Do

Art Meets Urban Culture

Every Olympus piece is a collaboration — between designer and artist, tradition and rebellion, the local and the global. We don't follow trends. We document culture, stitch it into fabric, and put it on the streets where it belongs.

Our Approach

Ethical by Design

We are committed to a circular economy. Every garment is produced ethically, using recycled fabrics where possible, and shipped in zero-waste packaging. Sustainability isn't a feature — it's our foundation.

Our Reach

Local Craft, Global Scale

We partner with artisans and makers from across the globe, elevating local craftsmanship to an international stage. From embroidery houses to independent graphic artists, every collaborator is credited and fairly compensated.

FAQ

What sizes does Olympus offer?
We offer XS through 3XL across all garment categories. Size guides are available on each product page. Olympus cuts tend to run oversized — we recommend sizing down if you prefer a more fitted silhouette.
Are your drops truly limited edition?
Yes. Every Olympus collection is produced in a limited run. Once a piece sells out, it does not restock. This ensures that every garment remains rare, meaningful, and exclusive to those who move fast.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship globally to over 80 countries. Standard shipping takes 7–14 business days internationally. Express options are available at checkout. All orders are shipped in our signature zero-waste packaging.
How do I care for my Olympus garment?
Cold wash, inside out, on a gentle cycle. Hang dry or tumble dry on low. Do not iron directly on printed or embroidered areas. Following these steps will preserve the integrity of your piece for years.
Can I collaborate or submit artwork?
Absolutely. Olympus is always seeking new creative voices. Send your portfolio and a short proposal to our contact page. We review all submissions and will respond within 14 days.
